Problem Statement

In today's health-conscious society, individuals strive to maintain a healthy lifestyle through regular physical activity. However, accurately estimating the number of calories burned during workouts can be challenging due to the varying personal attributes and nature of physical activities. Without precise calorie burn predictions, individuals may struggle to optimize their fitness routines and achieve their health goals.

Project Description

The objective of this project is to develop a machine learning model that accurately predicts the calories burned during physical activities. The model will utilize user-specific information (e.g., age, gender, weight, height) and activity-related features (e.g., duration, heart rate, activity type) to provide reliable calorie burn estimates. By leveraging machine learning techniques, the model aims to help users better understand their energy expenditure, allowing them to tailor their fitness routines effectively and achieve their fitness goals.

Project Steps

  • Data Collection: Gathering relevant data on personal attributes and activity metrics.
  • Data Preprocessing: Cleaning and preparing the data for model training.
  • Feature Engineering: Selecting and creating relevant features to enhance model performance.
  • Model Development: Training and testing various machine learning models for calorie prediction.
  • Evaluation: Assessing the model's accuracy and reliability using appropriate metrics.
  • Deployment: Creating a user-friendly interface to deliver predictions.

This solution aims to empower individuals with accurate insights into their workout effectiveness, promoting healthier and more efficient fitness practices.
