India Suicides Analysis 2001-2012

Project Description

This Power BI project focuses on analyzing suicide trends in India from 2001 to 2012. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of suicide data, segmented by gender, age group, and types of suicides. The analysis also highlights annual trends and comparative insights to help identify key factors contributing to suicides during the period.

Key Features of the Dashboard

Total Suicides Overview

  • Displays the total number of suicides reported between 2001 and 2012.
  • Includes a breakdown by gender (male and female).

Suicides by Age Groups

  • Provides a detailed view of suicides categorized into various age groups (e.g., <15 years, 15-29 years, 30-44 years, etc.).
  • Visualized through bar or pie charts for better understanding.

Top 10 Suicide Types

  • Highlights the ten most common reasons/types of suicides (e.g., family issues, illness, unemployment).
  • Includes percentage contributions of each type.

Bottom 10 Suicide Types

  • Lists the ten least common reasons/types of suicides during the same period.

Yearly Trends

  • Tracks annual suicide trends, showing the rise or decline in cases over the years.
  • Includes separate visualizations for total suicides and gender-specific trends.

Insights and Analysis Tools

  • Interactive filters for drilling down by year, gender, age group, and suicide type.
  • Comparisons between male and female suicides over the years.
  • Identification of significant patterns and anomalies in the data.

Tools and Techniques Used

  • Data Import and Cleaning: Power Query
  • Data Visualization: Power BI visuals such as bar charts, pie charts, and line graphs
  • Summary Reports: KPI cards for quick insights into totals and trends
