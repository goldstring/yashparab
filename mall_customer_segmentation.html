Customer Segmentation using K-Means

Project Resources

  • Kaggle Notebook : Click Here

Problem Statement:

In an e-commerce platform or retail environment, understanding customer purchasing behavior is key to offering personalized services and optimizing product offerings. Traditional methods of market analysis often fail to capture intricate patterns in customer data. This project focuses on using unsupervised machine learning techniques, specifically K-Means clustering, to perform Market Basket Analysis and Customer Segmentation. By analyzing customer behavior and purchasing patterns, the project aims to identify distinct customer segments based on their interactions with the platform. These segments can be used to score customers and tailor marketing strategies, product recommendations, and inventory management to improve overall customer satisfaction and sales performance.

Project Description:

This project applies K-Means clustering, an unsupervised learning technique, to perform a comprehensive market basket analysis and segment customers based on their purchasing behaviors. The process involves the following key steps:

Data Collection:

Gather customer transaction data, including purchase history, frequency, and item details.

Data Preprocessing:

Clean and preprocess the data by handling missing values, converting categorical data into numerical format, and normalizing values for clustering.

Feature Engineering:

Identify key features that represent customer behavior, such as product categories purchased, average transaction value, and frequency of purchases.

Clustering:

Implement K-Means clustering to group customers with similar purchasing patterns. Determine the optimal number of clusters using techniques like the Elbow Method.

Segmentation:

Categorize customers into different segments based on cluster results. These segments may represent high-value customers, frequent buyers, bargain hunters, or seasonal shoppers.

Scoring:

Assign scores to customers within each segment based on their purchasing behavior, allowing for targeted marketing and personalized recommendations.

Evaluation:

Use silhouette scores or other clustering evaluation metrics to assess the effectiveness of the segmentation.

By leveraging K-Means clustering, this project can help businesses better understand their customers, improve product placement, optimize marketing strategies, and enhance overall customer engagement.

This solution can be easily adapted to different industries, including retail, hospitality, and e-commerce, to drive customer-centric strategies and increase business profitability.
